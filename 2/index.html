Project 1: Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii Collection

Overview

I split each tall plate into three grayscale slices (top→bottom = B, G, R), aligned G and R to B via normalized cross-correlation (with an image pyramid for large scans), then stacked into RGB. Borders were trimmed for scoring and lightly cropped for the final display.

Method (single-scale + pyramid with edges)

  • Similarity: NCC on the cropped interior; optional edge magnitude improves robustness.
  • Single-scale: brute force shifts in a local window.
  • Pyramid: align at coarse scale, scale shifts up, refine with a small window.
